Facilities Management

Facilities Management

provides aspects of facility management that includes planning, evaluating and maintaining building systems. We offer three basic areas:

  • Soft Facilities Management (Soft FM)
  • Hard Facilities
  • Management (Hard FM)
  • IoT Facilities Management

Soft Facilities Management (Soft FM)

We offer facilities management services that a building or business relies on to maintain a pleasant, healthy and safe working environment. These are services that make sure that the building is well maintained and thus enable our clients workplace to run efficiently.

Soft FM services include:

  • Cleaning - Commercial and industrial cleaning, window cleaning and drainage services.
  • Waste Management - Waste collection, transportation, disposal and monitoring of waste.
  • Security Services - Businesses of all sizes and in all industries should be proactively protecting their building, assets and occupants. Security services can include physical security (like fences), CCTV/, technology solutions (like access control and intruder alarms) and key holding services.
  • Transportation - Under facilities management, the transportation sector is responsible for a wide range of services for their employees, including the purchase, repair, and maintenance of the number of company vehicles. It also provides guidelines for the safety in the operation of these vehicles, among others.

Other services offered under Soft FM include:

  • Building Maintenance
  • Adhoc Maintenance
  • Painting/decorating
  • Plumbing Maintenance
  • Electrical Maintenance
  • Air-conditioning Maintenance
  • Civil Works Maintenance
  • Carpentry Maintenance
  • Diesel Generator Maintenance
  • High rise window cleaning
  • Exterior Cleaning
  • Grounds Maintenance
  • Hygiene Services
  • Pest Control
  • Security (Technology, Risk Management)

Hard Facilities Management (Hard FM)

Hard FM services relate to the physical structure of the building. They serve the built environment to provide support and security processes to the people. They affect the safety and welfare of our clients’ employees and visitors It also aims to improve the productivity of the employees by monitoring the physical properties of an organisation. These aspects are usually permanent and cannot be removed. Mupo also involves huge projects requiring facilities management within the organisation to support its core business without problems. Hard FM services in buildings usually cover:

  • Airconditioning (Heating and Cooling) systems
  • HVAC systems
  • Electrical and lighting
  • Wastewater and plumbing
  • Ventilation
  • Mechanical Systems

IoT Facilities Management

Maintaining and monitoring the state of corporate office buildings, warehouses, manufacturing facilities, and other objects is challenging and time-consuming, especially if all the related tasks are handled manually. Regardless of company managers’ skills and experience, the risk of errors and unexpected events remains high, as the human factor always has an influence on processes and decisions.

The core business that is handled by our facilities management team covers the following facilities management based through technology:

  • Data Analytics and Reporting
  • Data storage
  • Improved data and cybersecurity
  • Security and access control
  • Service management
  • CCTV Monitoring
  • HVAC Automation & Control
  • Smart Lighting Control
  • Central heating and air-conditioning
  • Fire suppression systems
  • Predictive Maintenance
  • Stock monitoring and usage
  • Smart energy meters
