Security & Training

Mupo Security & Training (S&T) is a trusted and reputable Security & Training service provider with extensive experience within the security industry.

For your peace of mind, our team is made of security officers that are PSiRA compliant, with comprehensive insurance and all locally required licenses/certifications.

In addition, for our training offering, Our Facilitators, Assessors and Moderators are PSIRA, PFTC and SASSETA accredited.

We remain committed to doing business by building partnerships with our customers to ensure enduring relationships. We are committed to providing the highest level of security services to ensure the safety and well-being of your organization.

Security and Training services:

We offer Security and Training services, including but not limited to:
  • Armed & Unarmed Guarding
  • Armed & Unarmed Reaction Response
  • Residential, Retail & Industrial
  • Special Events & Access Control
  • Control of Asset Removal
  • Close Protection / VIP
  • National Key Point
  • Crowd Control & Surveillance
  • PSiRA Grades Training
  • Firearm & Competency Training

Our Mission

Is to offer top-notch, customized security services offerings that will protect your assets, employees, and customers while minimizing risks and vulnerabilities. With our highly trained staff, state-of-the-art technology, and proven track record, we are confident that we can ensure the safety and security of your organization.

Our Vision

To provide reliable and effective integrated security services to our clients, with the goal of protecting their assets, properties, and people.

Our Objectives

Enhance the overall security of the premises.

  • Protect company assets, employees, and clients.
  • Prevent unauthorized access and security breaches.
  • Provide real-time monitoring and quick response to incidents promote a secure work environment.
